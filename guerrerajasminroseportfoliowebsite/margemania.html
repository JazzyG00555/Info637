THE MARGE MANIA COMICS

Overview

This was the first website I ever designed. I made it to showcase my comics strip, The Marge Mania, and my hobby of making comics.

The website consists of three pages. This includes the main page, the about page and the comic gallery page. Each page also provides links to The Marge Mania Comics other accounts such as Instagram and Webtoons.

Duration: 3 weeks

Year: 2024

Design Considerations

1. Main Page

Shows a featured image of the most recent comic made and links to the next pages.

2. About Page

This page contains information about the creator of the comic strip, their director's statement and a table of their past work.

3. Comic Gallery Page

This page shows a series of comics that have been posted back to back, allowing users to scroll and read through them.

Reflections

What Worked Well

  • The making of the table and placement of the picture in the about page were good challenges to improve my skills.
  • The linking of images was at first difficult, but with time became easier to do.

What Did Not Work Well

  • I forgot to place back buttons or links to bring users back to the original page. This is an element that is necessary and should be implemented in future projects.
