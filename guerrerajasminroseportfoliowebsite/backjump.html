BACKJUMP

Synopsis

In a cyberfunk cityscape, a group of graffiti artists perform their escape from their robotic bull captors in a journey of skate, danger, and friendship.

Film Festival Appearance: TIFF

Production Process

1. Character Development

After multiple character design iterations, three characters were created known as Black Sheep, Thief and Knight. We then developed the antagonists after being inspired by robotics and bulls.

2. 3D Backgrounds and Assets

The backgrounds were created in Unreal Engine, with the assets being drawn in Flipaclip and then being placed over animated scenes.

3. Animation and Coloring

Every scene was drawn with a chalk like line texure. Scenes were then animated and drawn in Procreate and Flipaclip. The scenes were then exported as PNG image sequences and colored in Krita.
