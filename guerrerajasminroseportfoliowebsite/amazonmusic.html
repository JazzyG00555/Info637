AMAZON MUSIC CUSTOMER RETENTION

Overview

How might we gamify Amazon Music so that we improve user retention and provide a unique music streaming experience?

Tune In

A way to interact with your music streaming service so you can personalize your profile and earn rewards.

Duration: 4 weeks

Team Members: Shasteny Maclang and Federico Sarno

Contributions: User Research and UX Design

Design Considerations

1. Listen to Music

Having an Amazon Music account and listening to music gets you rewards as you complete missions.

2. Personalize Profile

Make your profile your own by adding the badges and banners you unlock when you complete your missions.

3. Enjoy Rewards

Go to your missions page and see what extra goodies you unlocked with your missions, maybe there’s a free trial or a discount waiting for you.

Research

Customer Demographics

What do Users Think?

  • 64% have Prime
  • 84% have not used Amazon Music
  • 76% subscribe to a music streaming service
  • 45.8% would upgrade their subscription tier if incentives were offered.
  • 44% are not completely satisfied with their streaming service of choice.
  • 41.7% would use their streaming service more if it had more personalization features.
  • 20.8% would increase their usage if they had access to exclusive content from artists.

Userflow

Information Architecture

Feature Developments

  • Missions: Missions are challenges the user can complete to receive badges or banners to personalize their Amazon music experience.
  • Unlocking Rewards: More listening time, creating playlists and additional missions (listen to X hours in a week to unlock Y, or gain points to unlock Z)
  • Banners: Banners are also trophies that can be collected and then displayed on users pages to further show how big of a fan they are of certain artists.
  • Badges: Badges have three levels (Gold, Silver and Bronze) and will be like trophies the user can then display on their page.

Future Scope

1. Further Integration with Amazon Ecosystems

Services such as Amazon Music Live and rewards obtained from Twitch Drops are automatically tied to one's Amazon Music account. There could be further implementation of badges and banners within these areas.

2. Features

Listening Metrics would allow us to see what part of the customer retention reward system is the most effective. This would allow us to further develop the parts of the TuneIn reward system that are most efficient.

3. More Rewards:

Developing more badges and banners for Podcasts & Audiobooks as well as avatar decorations could be implemented.
