The Andrea Doria's Last Message

Synopsis

An Italian grandmother tells the story of her immigration to North America on the Andrea Doria, a boat, which later sank during the voyage she was originally supposed to be on. This film is based on true events.

Production Process

1. Scanning and Collecting

I began scanning the letters and postcards early on so that I could begin organizing them and analyzing the textures/images that were made available to me. A light table was used to create diverse textures by overlapping multiple letters over one another.

2. Backgrounds and Rigging

The backgrounds and any props were drawn in Procreate and then exported into After Effects as separate PNG files. I began rigging the character’s limbs and clothes by using a combination of DUIK Bassel 2 and Puppet pins.

3. Animating!

I would put part of the animatic over my rigged characters, decrease the animatic’s opacity and would turn the animatic layer on and off so that I could follow the scene as needed as I animated. I also began parenting objects to my characters hands whenever my character was holding an object in the scene. I did this to make sure the object would follow my character hand movement accordingly.
